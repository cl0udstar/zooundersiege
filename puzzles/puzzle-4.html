Accessibility

Next, we must get the zoo wide IOT system back operational, so that the sensor readings can inform the keepers what is happening.

Two sensor suppliers have lent the zoo their sensors to check which are best. The keepers have kept a note of the Radio Signal Strength Indicator (RSSI) at all points across the zoo. It is your job to help the keepers to decide which sensors are best, Alpha or Beta. (note that a higher signal which is less negative is best. Ask your teacher to explain this if you are unsure.)
